# WorkTrack

WorkTrack is an Android-based employee management application designed to simplify workplace administration by integrating attendance tracking, leave management, and reimbursement processes into a single platform.

The idea originated from observing real operational challenges during discussions with a company, where attendance, leave requests, and reimbursement submissions were still managed through separate manual processes. This often resulted in delayed reporting, limited visibility of leave balances, and inefficient communication between employees and HR teams.

WorkTrack aims to improve efficiency, transparency, and accessibility by providing a centralized digital solution for employees, managers, finance teams, and HR personnel.

---

## Problem Statement

Many organizations still manage employee administration through disconnected workflows.

Common challenges include:

* Manual attendance reporting
* Leave requests submitted through multiple communication channels
* Difficulty tracking leave balances and leave history
* Reimbursement processes requiring repetitive communication
* Limited transparency for employees and management

These challenges inspired the development of WorkTrack as a unified employee service platform.

---

## Solution

WorkTrack centralizes employee administration into a single Android application that enables:

* Attendance tracking
* Leave management
* Reimbursement submission
* Approval workflows
* Administrative monitoring

The platform is designed to create a smoother experience for both employees and administrators.
